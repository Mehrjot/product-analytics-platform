# Product Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end Product Analytics and Business Intelligence solution built using SQL and Power BI.

The goal of the project is to analyze customer behavior, product performance, revenue trends, retention patterns, and customer lifetime value using real-world e-commerce data.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow:

* Data Exploration
* SQL-Based Business Analysis
* KPI Development
* Customer Segmentation (RFM)
* Cohort Analysis
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Interactive Power BI Dashboards

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Drivers

* Beauty & Health generated the highest revenue.
* Watches & Gifts ranked among the highest-performing categories.
* Home & Bath products recorded the highest sales volume.

### Customer Insights

* Most customers belong to the Potential Loyalists segment.
* A significant customer group is classified as At Risk.
* Champion customers represent a small but highly valuable segment.

### Product Insights

* Premium product categories command significantly higher average prices.
* Revenue concentration is driven by a limited number of categories.

### Retention Insights

* Customer acquisition increased steadily over time.
* Customer retention opportunities exist within the At-Risk segment.
